Highlighted Services

We strive to provide optimal health and wellness for our patients through individualized care. We give each of our patients the highest level of attention and time to address their unique health care needs. Ethical and evidence based medicine is our top priority. Through these ideals, Dr. Burke will provide integrative medicine that goes beyond the boundaries of conventional medicine.

Thyroid Health

With the aid of lab testing, we can measure a baseline of your thyroid’s health and then create a plan to manage and improve your thyroid’s function. Check out this video to learn the basics!

Hormone Balancing

Hormone imbalances are common but should not be considered normal! Dr. Burke will help guide you and offer the best options available to balance your hormones.

Adrenal Support

Dr. Burke can discuss herbal supplementation and lifestyle changes to help rejuvenate your adrenals.

Digestive Health

Digestive Health doesn’t always end in the gut itself. Hormones, stress, lifestyle can all impact the gastrointestinal system, this is why finding the root cause of digestive health is incredibly important. Dr. Burke works with her patients to take back control of their digestive health.

Women’s Medicine

Diagnose and treat common women’s health issues through integrative methods and alternative treatments. Dr. Burke is here for her patients through all of life’s transitions.

Menopause Consult

Are you curious about what options are available to you for your menopausal symptoms? Are you looking for more information on bio-identical hormones or hormone replacement therapy? Or perhaps you are curious about non-hormonal options? Let Dr. Burke present all the options available to you!

Meet Dr. Burke

Dr. Tara Burke

Naturopathic Medical Doctor

Dr. Tara Burke is a licensed Naturopathic Doctor in Scottsdale, Arizona. Dr. Burke’s primary focus is in women’s medicine and endocrinology, having received extensive training in thyroid health, adrenal health, and hormone balance after graduation from the Southwest College of Naturopathic Medicine. She spends time with her patients to identify their individual health goals and treat, not just the symptoms, but the whole person. She utilizes lifestyle, nutrition, supplementation, herbals, acupuncture, and when indicated, medications, to find a solution for each of her patients. Ethical, evidence-based medicine is her top priority. Dr. Burke is currently practicing at Blossom Wellness in North Scottsdale, Arizona near to the Grayhawk, DC Ranch, Desert Ridge, and Pinnacle Peak areas. Click Here to Schedule.
